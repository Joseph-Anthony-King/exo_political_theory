[h1]Exo Political Theory[/h1]

This mod is based on [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=848053326]Test23456's[/url] [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=848053326]Influence Generator[/url] mod but has been adjusted to focus on influence and diplomacy.

This mod contains a new technology and a new building:

[b]Exo Political Theory:[/b] Our classical political theories must be updated to account for alien psychologies.

[b]Exo Affairs Institute:[/b] An institute of public and private specialists to coordinate our external affairs and spread our values and influence.

The [b]Exo Affairs Institute[/b] is limited to one on the capital planet and it's stats are as follows:

[h2]Requirements[/h2]
[list]
[*]Is the capital of an empire
[*]Limit of 1
[*]Requires planet population of 10
[*]5000 minerals
[/list]

[h2]Upkeep[/h2]
[list]
[*]8 energy units per month
[*]4 consumer goods per month
[/list]

[h2]Benefits[/h2]
[list]
[*]2 influence points per month
[*]Adds an envoy
[/list]

[h2]Jobs[/h2]

[*]2 bureaucrat jobs
[/list]
